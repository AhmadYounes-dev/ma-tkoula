ما تقولها (Ma T'oulha) — Party Game Privacy Policy & Support · Last updated: July 18, 2026 Privacy Policy Support Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game ما تقولها (Ma T'oulha) ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. 1. Information We Collect The App is designed to be played locally and does not require you to create an account. We do not ask for your name, phone number, or address. However, some information is collected automatically to run ads and process purchases: Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as an advertising ID), approximate (non-precise) location, IP address, and app-usage information to serve and measure ads. Purchase data: When you buy premium content, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your credit card or full payment details; we only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed. Support requests: If you email us, we receive the email address and any information you choose to include so we can respond. 2. How We Use Information To display advertisements within the App. To process and restore in-app purchases. To respond to your support questions and feedback. To detect, prevent, and fix technical issues. 3. Advertising The App displays ads through Google AdMob. Because the App is family-friendly, we serve only non-personalized ads to users who may be children. Non-personalized ads are based on general context rather than a user's past behavior. Learn more here: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services. 4. In-App Purchases The App offers optional premium content through in-app purchases. All payments are handled securely by Google Play. Purchases can be restored from the Premium Content page using the same Google Account used for the original purchase. 5. Children's Privacy The App is intended to be family-friendly and may be used by children. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Only non-personalized ads are shown to children. The App does not include social features that let children share personal information publicly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below and we will delete it promptly. 6. Information is shared only with the service providers listed above, and only as needed to run ads and process purchases, or where required by law. 8. Data Retention & Security We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here. We use reasonable technical measures to protect information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. 9. Your Rights Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete information related to you.
